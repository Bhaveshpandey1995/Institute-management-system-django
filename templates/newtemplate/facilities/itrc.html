Central Facilies ITRC

Vision


Our vision is to create an state of art intranet which enables the sharing of knowledge base round the clock through wider internet bandwidth.
Download ITRC Facilities

Objectives


  • To provide inter connectivity among different departments and enhance existing facility to access internet worldwide effectively through a 2 Mbps dedicated leased line connectivity.
  • To provide central computing facilities to student and staff for their learning, classroom teaching and research needs.
  • To strengthen the PG Programme in different departments.
  • To conduct short / long term intensive training programme to the working professionals as per industry/ institutions needs.
  • To prepare and disseminate the instructional material related to design methodologies (viz. VLSI design, CAD design, Multimedia based design etc.)
  • To provide LAN connection to faculty ,cabins faculty residences and hostels to access centralized facility available in the resource center round the clock
  • To offer a variety of training programmes on all emerging areas of computing and engineering design.
  • To provide specialized design software's to cater the specific needs of different departments.
